How I Think
01 — NOTICE

Something's off

A pacing report looks a little strange, or a client flags a number that doesn't match what they're seeing.

02 — DIAGNOSE

Find where it splits

Pull the report, check the tag, compare first- and third-party numbers — find exactly where reality splits from the plan.

03 — FIX

Adjust what's causing it

Targeting, budget, creative rotation, or a tag fix — whatever the diagnosis points to, and loop in the right team if it's not mine to fix alone.

04 — CONFIRM

Check, don't assume

Re-check delivery after the fix goes live. It isn't resolved until the numbers actually say so.

Same loop either way — an ad server issue or a bidding platform issue. The specifics change; the process doesn't.
